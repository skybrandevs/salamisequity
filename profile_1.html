Abimbola Ajinibi

Vice President, FBN Quest Trustees (NG).

Abimbola Ajinibi holds a second-class upper bachelor’s degree from the University of Ibadan and
thereafter obtained MSc in International Management from the University of London.

Bimbo is currently a Vice President with FBNQuest Trustees (A member of FBN Holding Company) as the Regional Head in charge of the company business in the Northern Region. Prior to joining FBN Trustees, he was a Relationship Manager with Fountain Trust Bank Plc, Nigeria. He started his Banking career with United Bank of Africa Plc in 1998, where he acquired tremendous experience in Retail, Commercial and Corporate Banking.

Bimbo is a seasoned professional with over 20 years’ experience in Financial Services, Sales and Business Strategy. He has attended various courses including at Harvard University and MIT Sloan School of Management for Executive Education studies in Leadership and Negotiation from Harvard University.

Abimbola also attended training on Governance at Singapore Management University. Bimbo is a Public Analyst with a passion for a better Nigeria and he enjoy playing Golf.
