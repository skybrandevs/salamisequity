James Watson

Director, James Hardley Building Materials (USA).

James H. Watson is the Director of Sales for James Hardie Building Products, Inc., an industry leading building materials company. Prior to joining James Hardie, Mr. Watson served as a sales leader at Electrolux Major Appliances, a global consumer durables manufacturer.

After a decade of leading diverse teams within the North American home-building industry, Mr. Watson has established himself as an inspirational leader of people who drives strategic corporate direction with executional excellence.

He completed his undergraduate studies with a B.S. in Business Administration from the University of North Carolina at Wilmington and also an Executive MBA from the University of Texas at Arlington
