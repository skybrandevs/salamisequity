Our Approach

Our core values are built around the #SalamisEthos; a set of 7 principles that have shaped
some of the best-run and most successful organizations around the world.

Our Vision

To become the leading investment firm sponsoring and managing
investments across Sub-Sahara Africa and North America markets.
SELFLESS SERVICE

At #Salamis, we believe that service is the rent we pay for the life we live. We put the welfare of the global economy, Board of Directors, and our employees before our individual goals. Selfless service leads to organizational teamwork and encompasses discipline, self-control and faith in the system.

ACCOUNTABILITY

Our answerability, blameworthiness, liability, and the expectation of account-giving will be second to none. As a global brand, we will live up to the acknowledgment and assumption of responsibility for actions, products, decisions, and policies including the administration, governance, and implementation within the scope of the role or employment position and encompassing the obligation to report, explain and be answerable for resulting consequences.

LOYALTY

We bear true faith and allegiance to our host country's constitution, our Board of Trustees, and other stakeholders. We are loyal to our host nation and its heritage.

ABSOLUTE RESPECT

We rely upon the GOLDEN RULE. How we consider others reflects upon each of us, both personally and as a professional organization

MINDLESS FRUGALITY

We spend less money than the average company. But we’re not miserly – or extreme with saving. We are less wasteful and more resourceful. We strive to strike a balance between spending and saving.

INTEGRITY

We do what is right, legally and morally. We are willing to do what is right even when no one is looking. It is our "moral compass", an inner voice. We shall strive to be false-less, not fault-less. Mistakes are part of the journey. We shall strive to always own our mistakes.
